Information Warfare Summit!

Located at theMoore Norman Technology Center in southside OKC.


Information Warfare Summit (IWS) is a conference that has been going on in the OKC area now for 10 years. It is a collaboration now between ISSA OKC andInfraGardThis conference happens every year during the fall period, Oct/Nov timeframe. Join today to get in on the latest and greatest updates to this conference or if you are interested in helping out!

Organized by  local Security Community Leaders with the support of the Techlahoma Foundationand ISSA OKC.
